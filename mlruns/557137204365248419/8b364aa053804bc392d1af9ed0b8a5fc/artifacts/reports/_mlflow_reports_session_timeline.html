### TC-US09-01 — Valid Login
**Type:** Manual  |  **Priority:** Medium  
**Tags:** login, valid_credentials

**Description**  
Verify that a user with valid credentials can successfully login.

**Preconditions**
- A user account with valid email and password exists.

**Test Steps**
1. Navigate to the Automation Test Store homepage.
2. Click on the 'Login or register' link.
3. Enter a valid email address in the 'Email' field.
4. Enter a valid password in the 'Password' field.
5. Click the 'Login' button.

**Expected Result**
> The user is successfully logged in and redirected to the My Account dashboard.

**Tags:** login, invalid_email
