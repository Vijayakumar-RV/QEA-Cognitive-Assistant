### US05-TC01 — Verify increasing item quantity in the cart
**Type:**   |  **Priority:** High  
**Tags:** cart, quantity, update

**Description**  
This test case verifies that a user can increase the quantity of an item in the shopping cart and that the cart total updates accordingly.

**Preconditions**
- User is on the Home Page (https://www.automationteststore.com/).
- At least one item is present in the cart.

**Test Steps**
1. Navigate to the Home Page.
2. Click on the 'Cart' link in the header.
3. **MISSING INFORMATION:** The context does not provide any UI elements or steps for interacting with item quantities (e.g., input fields, increase buttons) within the cart page.
4. **MISSING INFORMATION:** The context does not provide any UI elements or steps to verify the updated quantity or the dynamic update of the cart total.

**Expected Result**
> The quantity of the selected item should increase, and the cart total should update dynamically to reflect the new quantity.
